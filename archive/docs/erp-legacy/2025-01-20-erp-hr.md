## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Overview
The HR Module is a comprehensive human resources management system that streamlines all HR processes from recruitment to offboarding. It provides tools for employee management, payroll processing, performance tracking, and compliance management.

### 1.2 Business Value
- Streamlined HR operations through automation
- Improved employee experience with self-service portal
- Better compliance with labor laws and regulations
- Data-driven HR decisions with advanced analytics
- Reduced administrative overhead
- Enhanced talent management and retention

### 1.3 Target Users
- HR Managers
- Employees
- Department Managers
- Payroll Administrators
- Recruiters
- C-Level Executives

## 2. Current State vs Future State
